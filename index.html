A44 Engineering

Control Systems & Hybrid Platforms

A44 Engineering specialises in safety-conscious control systems, hybrid traction and embedded software for complex machines.

Core capabilities

  • IQAN MC43/MD4 architecture and logic development
  • Hybrid traction logic, HV bus & inverter control
  • BMS logic – SoC estimation, thermal & protection
  • CAN J1939 frameworks, diagnostics & DBC design
  • HMI/UI development (Qt, MD4/MD5)
  • System-level debugging, EMC/CAN noise investigation

Industries

  • Hybrid/Electric locomotives
  • Off-highway & agricultural machinery
  • Industrial automation & hydraulics
  • EV/Hybrid R&D platforms
  • Robotics & prototype systems
